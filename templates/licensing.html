Quick Order Licensing

1. Why License?

By licensing Quick Order to independent venues—restaurants, pubs, food trucks—you provide them an instant, secure local ordering system. No app-install, no postage, just QR-driven menus and dashboard.

2. Suggested Pricing Tiers

  • Basic: £20/mo — Up to 2 tablets
  • Pro: £50/mo — Up to 5 tablets + monthly analytics
  • Enterprise: £100/mo — Unlimited devices + priority support

3. Revenue Projection

# Venues Basic (£20) Pro (£50) Enterprise (£100) Total Monthly
10 £200 £500 £1000 £1,700
50 £1,000 £2,500 £5,000 £8,500
100 £2,000 £5,000 £10,000 £17,000

4. Getting Started

  1. Sign up your first pilot venue at the Basic tier.
  2. Collect feedback and refine installation docs.
  3. Offer volume discounts or annual pre-pay plans.
  4. Upsell to Pro/Enterprise as they scale operations.
Estimate: with just 50 paying venues at a mix of tiers, you could net £8.5k per month—over £100k per year—on minimal infrastructure!
