2. Strategies of Commerce

  • Drop-Shipping: Partner with wholesalers—no warehouse overhead.
  • White-Labeling: Your own crest upon generic hardware, commanding premium.
  • Affiliate Programs: Earn 10-20% on cables, boosters, printers—an ancillary bounty.
  • Bundled Discounts: Offer “Add-On” kits at 15% off to upsell existing clients.
  • Tiered Licensing: Base system + hardware package—forged into an iron-clad contract.
