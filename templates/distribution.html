Distribution Plan for Quick Order System

1. Initial Local Deployment

  • Start by offering free trials to local independent restaurants, cafes, and pop-ups.
  • Create physical media: demo kits, brochures, business cards and leave-behinds.
  • Host a demo event or table in food festivals or business expos.
  • Leverage QR-coded demonstration sites (like hosted on GitHub Pages).

2. Digital Distribution Channels

  • Use Vercel or Render for hosting online demos with live sandbox environments.
  • Launch a small business site on Squarespace or Webflow showcasing features, videos, pricing tiers and support.
  • Build landing pages for each product: Quick Order App, Dashboard, Admin Tools, AR Showcase.
  • Create video walkthroughs for YouTube and LinkedIn.

3. Partnerships & Sales

  • Partner with POS vendors, restaurant supply companies, and local business associations.
  • Offer commission or affiliate rewards for referrals by influencers or tech-savvy restaurant consultants.
  • Sell bundles through Amazon, Shopify, or food-tech marketplaces.

4. Hardware Distribution

  • Create SKU-based bundles with printers, tablets, signage, and WiFi extenders.
  • Drop-shipping: use Alibaba, Printful, or Spocket.
  • Consider fulfillment using ShipBob or Amazon FBA.

5. Scaling the Business

  • Start with subscriptions for individual locations: £20–£50/mo.
  • Upsell premium support, analytics dashboard, AR item hosting, white-labelling.
  • Introduce license tiers for multi-branch restaurants or hotel chains.
  • Offer co-branded kits to resellers, like IT installers or hospitality equipment suppliers.

6. Who to Contact

  • Restaurant owners and managers via cold email, in-person visits, and trade events.
  • Hospitality tech vendors for OEM/white label partnerships.
  • Freelance business developers on platforms like Upwork or Fiverr.

7. Final Thoughts

This app has the flexibility to scale from pubs and food trucks to large venue chains. A hybrid offering of software and hardware makes it easier to justify higher prices and sell complete business solutions.
